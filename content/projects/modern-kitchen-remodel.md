# Modern Kitchen Transformation

This stunning kitchen remodel completely transformed a dated 1990s kitchen into a modern culinary masterpiece. The project showcases our expertise in custom cabinetry, stone work, and space optimization while maintaining the home's original character.

## Project Overview

**Location**: Builder City, CA
**Timeline**: 6 weeks
**Budget Range**: $75,000 - $100,000
**Square Footage**: 280 sq ft

## The Challenge

The original kitchen was cramped, outdated, and lacked the functionality needed for modern family life. Key issues included:

- Limited counter space and storage
- Outdated appliances and fixtures
- Poor lighting and ventilation
- Closed-off layout that isolated the cook from family activities
- Worn cabinets and laminate countertops

## Our Solution

We completely reimagined the space while working within the existing footprint, creating a bright, functional kitchen that serves as the heart of the home.

## Project Highlights

### Custom Cabinetry

- **Soft-Close White Shaker Cabinets**: Timeless design with modern functionality
- **Crown Molding & Trim**: Elegant details that add sophistication
- **Pull-Out Drawers**: Maximized storage with easy access to items
- **Under-Cabinet LED Lighting**: Task lighting for food preparation

### Premium Surfaces

- **Quartz Countertops**: Durable Caesarstone surfaces with waterfall edge on island
- **Subway Tile Backsplash**: Classic 3x6 subway tiles with decorative border accent
- **Luxury Vinyl Plank Flooring**: Water-resistant flooring that mimics hardwood
- **Custom Tile Work**: Detailed tile installation around the range area

### Modern Appliances

- **Stainless Steel Package**: Coordinated appliances for a cohesive look
- **36" Gas Range**: Professional-style cooking with powerful burners
- **Built-In Microwave**: Space-saving design integrated into cabinetry
- **Counter-Depth Refrigerator**: Streamlined appearance that doesn't protrude
- **Quiet Dishwasher**: Energy-efficient model with multiple wash cycles

### Space Optimization

- **Removed Wall**: Created open concept connection to dining area
- **Large Center Island**: Added 8 feet of counter space and seating for 4
- **Floor-to-Ceiling Cabinets**: Maximized storage with upper cabinets to the ceiling
- **Dedicated Coffee Station**: Built-in area for coffee maker and supplies
- **Pantry Organization**: Custom shelving system for maximum storage efficiency

## Design Features

### Lighting Design

- **Pendant Lights**: Three coordinating pendants over the island
- **Recessed Lighting**: General lighting throughout the space
- **Under-Cabinet LEDs**: Task lighting for food preparation
- **Natural Light**: Enlarged window over sink for better views

### Color Palette

- **Crisp White Cabinets**: Bright, clean appearance that won't go out of style
- **Warm Gray Walls**: Subtle color that complements the white cabinetry
- **Stainless Steel Accents**: Modern touches through appliances and hardware
- **Natural Wood Tones**: Warm contrast through bar stools and accessories

### Storage Solutions

- **Deep Drawers**: Better access to pots, pans, and small appliances
- **Corner Solutions**: Lazy Susan and pull-out shelves in corner cabinets
- **Spice Rack**: Built-in spice storage near the cooking area
- **Trash Pull-Out**: Hidden waste and recycling bins

## Timeline & Process

**Week 1-2**: Demolition and Structural Work

- Removed old cabinets, countertops, and appliances
- Demolished wall between kitchen and dining room
- Updated electrical and plumbing rough-in

**Week 3-4**: Infrastructure and Preparation

- Installed new drywall and completed painting
- Installed luxury vinyl plank flooring
- Completed electrical and plumbing connections

**Week 5-6**: Installation and Finishing

- Installed custom cabinets and hardware
- Templated and installed quartz countertops
- Completed tile backsplash installation
- Installed and connected all appliances
- Final details and cleanup
