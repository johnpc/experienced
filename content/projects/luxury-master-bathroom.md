# Luxury Master Bathroom Retreat

This master bathroom renovation transformed a cramped, outdated space into a luxurious spa-like retreat. The project demonstrates our expertise in creating functional yet elegant bathrooms that serve as personal sanctuaries.

## Project Overview

**Location**: Craftsman Hills, CA
**Timeline**: 4 weeks
**Budget Range**: $45,000 - $60,000
**Square Footage**: 120 sq ft

## The Challenge

The original master bathroom was small, poorly lit, and lacked the luxury amenities the homeowners desired. Key issues included:

- Cramped layout with poor traffic flow
- Single vanity with limited storage
- Small shower with outdated tile
- Poor lighting and ventilation
- Lack of luxury features and spa-like atmosphere

## Our Solution

We completely reconfigured the space, borrowing square footage from an adjacent closet to create a more spacious and luxurious bathroom that feels like a high-end spa.

## Project Highlights

### Spa-Inspired Design

- **Freestanding Soaking Tub**: Elegant centerpiece for relaxation
- **Walk-In Shower**: Spacious shower with multiple shower heads
- **Natural Materials**: Marble and wood accents for warmth
- **Calming Color Palette**: Neutral tones that promote relaxation

### Custom Features

- **Double Vanity**: His and hers sinks with ample counter space
- **Custom Cabinetry**: Soft-close drawers with interior organizers
- **Built-In Storage**: Recessed niches and linen closet
- **Heated Floors**: Radiant floor heating for comfort

### Premium Finishes

- **Carrara Marble**: Natural stone surfaces throughout
- **Custom Tile Work**: Large format tiles with decorative accents
- **Brushed Gold Fixtures**: Warm metallic finishes for elegance
- **LED Lighting**: Energy-efficient lighting with dimming controls

## Design Features

### Layout Optimization

- **Expanded Footprint**: Borrowed 20 sq ft from adjacent closet
- **Improved Traffic Flow**: Better circulation around fixtures
- **Separate Zones**: Distinct areas for bathing, showering, and grooming
- **Privacy Considerations**: Strategic placement of fixtures and windows

### Luxury Amenities

- **Rainfall Shower Head**: 12-inch square rainfall shower head
- **Handheld Shower**: Adjustable handheld for flexibility
- **Body Sprays**: Multiple body sprays for spa-like experience
- **Steam Feature**: Steam generator for therapeutic benefits
- **Towel Warmers**: Heated towel racks for comfort

### Storage Solutions

- **Vanity Storage**: Deep drawers with custom organizers
- **Medicine Cabinets**: Recessed cabinets with LED lighting
- **Linen Closet**: Built-in storage for towels and toiletries
- **Shower Niches**: Multiple recessed shelves for bath products

## Material Selection

### Flooring

- **Large Format Porcelain**: 24x24 tiles that mimic natural stone
- **Heated Subfloor**: Electric radiant heating system
- **Slip-Resistant Finish**: Safety without compromising style

### Wall Surfaces

- **Carrara Marble**: Natural stone for shower and accent walls
- **Porcelain Tile**: Durable and water-resistant surfaces
- **Paint**: High-quality bathroom paint in calming neutral tones

### Fixtures & Hardware

- **Brushed Gold Finish**: Warm metallic throughout for consistency
- **High-Quality Fixtures**: Premium brands for durability and style
- **Soft-Close Hardware**: Quiet operation on all drawers and doors

## Timeline & Process

**Week 1**: Demolition and Structural Work

- Removed all existing fixtures and finishes
- Reconfigured plumbing and electrical systems
- Expanded room by removing closet wall
- Installed heated floor system

**Week 2**: Infrastructure and Waterproofing

- Completed rough plumbing and electrical work
- Installed proper waterproofing in shower area
- Completed drywall and painting
- Installed ventilation system

**Week 3**: Tile and Fixture Installation

- Installed floor and wall tile
- Set freestanding tub and plumbing connections
- Built custom shower with glass enclosure
- Installed vanity and countertops

**Week 4**: Finishing Touches

- Installed all fixtures and hardware
- Completed electrical connections and lighting
- Final plumbing connections and testing
- Cleanup and final walkthrough

## Innovative Solutions

### Space Expansion

We creatively expanded the bathroom by incorporating space from an underutilized closet, gaining valuable square footage without major structural changes.

### Lighting Design

- **Natural Light**: Enlarged existing window with privacy glass
- **Vanity Lighting**: LED strips integrated into mirror frames
- **Ambient Lighting**: Recessed lights with dimming controls
- **Accent Lighting**: LED strips in shower niches and toe kicks

### Ventilation

- **Quiet Exhaust Fan**: High-capacity, low-noise ventilation
- **Humidity Control**: Automatic humidity sensing controls
