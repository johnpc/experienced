# About Premier Construction & Remodeling

For over 20 years, Premier Construction & Remodeling has been transforming homes and businesses throughout California with exceptional craftsmanship and unwavering commitment to quality. Founded in 2003 by master craftsman Michael Johnson, our company has grown from a small family business to one of the region's most trusted construction and remodeling contractors.

## Our Story

What started as a passion for creating beautiful, functional spaces has evolved into a full-service construction company that handles everything from minor home improvements to major commercial renovations. Our founder, Michael Johnson, began his career as an apprentice carpenter and worked his way up through every aspect of the construction industry, gaining invaluable experience that now guides our company's approach to every project.

## Our Mission

At Premier Construction & Remodeling, our mission is simple: to exceed our clients' expectations through superior craftsmanship, honest communication, and reliable service. We believe that every project, regardless of size, deserves the same level of attention to detail and commitment to excellence.

## What Sets Us Apart

### Experience & Expertise

With over two decades in the industry, we've completed thousands of projects ranging from kitchen remodels to complete home renovations and commercial buildouts. Our team brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every job.

### Quality Craftsmanship

We take pride in our work and stand behind every project with comprehensive warranties. Our attention to detail and commitment to using quality materials ensures that your investment will last for years to come.

### Licensed & Insured

Premier Construction & Remodeling is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain all necessary certifications and stay current with building codes and industry best practices.

### Customer-Focused Approach

We understand that construction projects can be disruptive to your daily life. That's why we work closely with our clients to minimize inconvenience while keeping you informed throughout the entire process.

### Local Community

As a locally-owned business, we're invested in our community. We source materials from local suppliers when possible and take pride in contributing to the beauty and value of our neighborhoods.
