# Kitchen Remodeling Services

Transform the heart of your home with our comprehensive kitchen remodeling services. From minor updates to complete renovations, we bring your vision to life with quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.

## Our Kitchen Remodeling Services

### Custom Cabinetry

- **Design & Installation**: Custom cabinets designed to maximize your space and match your style
- **Cabinet Refacing**: Update existing cabinets with new doors, drawers, and hardware
- **Soft-Close Hardware**: Premium hinges and drawer slides for smooth, quiet operation
- **Storage Solutions**: Pull-out drawers, lazy Susans, and custom organizers

### Countertops & Surfaces

- **Quartz Countertops**: Durable, non-porous surfaces in hundreds of colors and patterns
- **Granite & Marble**: Natural stone surfaces with unique beauty and character
- **Custom Edge Profiles**: Waterfall edges, beveled edges, and decorative details
- **Professional Installation**: Precise templating and expert installation

### Complete Kitchen Renovations

- **Space Planning**: Optimize your kitchen layout for better flow and functionality
- **Structural Changes**: Wall removal, additions, and layout modifications
- **Electrical Updates**: New outlets, lighting circuits, and appliance connections
- **Plumbing Modifications**: Relocate sinks, add islands, and upgrade fixtures

### Appliance Integration

- **Built-In Appliances**: Seamless integration of refrigerators, dishwashers, and microwaves
- **Kitchen Islands**: Custom islands with seating, storage, and appliance integration
- **Range Hoods**: Stylish and functional ventilation solutions
- **Under-Cabinet Lighting**: LED lighting for task and ambient illumination

### 1. Initial Consultation

We start with a free in-home consultation to discuss your vision, needs, and budget. We'll assess your current space and provide initial recommendations.

Our design team creates detailed plans and 3D renderings of your new kitchen. We'll help you select materials, finishes, and appliances that fit your style and budget.

### 3. Permits & Approvals

Once construction is complete, we conduct a thorough walkthrough to ensure every detail meets our high standards and your expectations.
