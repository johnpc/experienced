# Home Additions

Expand your living space without the hassle of moving with our custom home addition services. Whether you need more bedrooms, a larger kitchen, or a dedicated home office, we create seamless additions that look like they were always part of your home.

## Our Home Addition Services

### Room Additions

- **Single Room Extensions**: Add a bedroom, office, or family room
- **Multi-Room Additions**: Expand multiple rooms or create open-concept spaces
- **Bump-Out Additions**: Extend existing rooms for more space
- **Custom Layouts**: Design additions that perfectly fit your lifestyle

### Second Story Additions

- **Full Second Story**: Double your home's square footage
- **Partial Second Story**: Add bedrooms and bathrooms above existing spaces
- **Structural Engineering**: Ensure your foundation can support the addition
- **Matching Architecture**: Seamlessly blend new construction with existing design

### Specialty Additions

- **Master Suite Additions**: Private retreats with bedroom, bathroom, and walk-in closet
- **Kitchen Extensions**: Expand your kitchen for better functionality and entertaining
- **In-Law Suites**: Self-contained living spaces for extended family
- **Home Office Additions**: Dedicated workspace separate from living areas

### Garage Conversions

- **Living Space Conversion**: Transform unused garage space into livable areas
- **ADU Creation**: Accessory dwelling units for rental income or family use
- **Workshop Conversion**: Create hobby spaces or home gyms
- **Storage Solutions**: Maintain storage while adding living space

## Types of Home Additions

### Horizontal Additions

Perfect when you have adequate lot space:

- **Side Additions**: Extend your home to the left or right
- **Rear Additions**: Add space to the back of your home
- **Wraparound Additions**: Extend multiple sides for maximum space
- **Detached Additions**: Separate structures connected by covered walkways

### Vertical Additions

Ideal for smaller lots or maximum space gain:

- **Second Story Additions**: Add an entire floor above existing structure
- **Dormer Additions**: Expand attic space with dormers
- **Loft Conversions**: Transform unused attic space into living areas
- **Roof Deck Additions**: Create outdoor living space above existing rooms

### Specialty Structures

- **Sunrooms & Conservatories**: Bring the outdoors in with glass-enclosed spaces
- **Screened Porches**: Enjoy outdoor living protected from insects
- **Three-Season Rooms**: Extend your living season with climate-controlled spaces
- **Pool Houses**: Create entertainment and changing areas near your pool

## The Home Addition Process

### 1. Initial Consultation & Site Assessment

We evaluate your property, discuss your needs, and assess the feasibility of your addition. We consider lot lines, setbacks, and existing utilities.

### 2. Design & Planning

Our design team creates detailed plans that complement your home's existing architecture. We ensure the addition flows naturally with your current layout.

### 3. Permits & Approvals

We handle all necessary permits, including building permits, zoning approvals, and utility connections. We ensure compliance with local building codes.

### 4. Structural Engineering

For larger additions, we work with structural engineers to ensure your home's foundation and framing can support the new construction.

### 5. Construction Phase

Our experienced team manages all aspects of construction, from foundation work to final finishes. We coordinate all trades and maintain quality control throughout.

### 6. Final Inspection & Walkthrough

We conduct thorough inspections and a final walkthrough to ensure your addition meets our high standards and your expectations.

## Design Considerations

### Architectural Harmony

We ensure your addition complements your home's existing style:

- **Matching Materials**: Use similar siding, roofing, and trim materials
- **Proportional Design**: Scale the addition appropriately to your home
- **Window Alignment**: Align new windows with existing ones
- **Roofline Integration**: Create seamless roofline transitions

### Functionality & Flow

We design additions that improve your home's overall functionality:

- **Traffic Patterns**: Ensure smooth flow between old and new spaces
- **Natural Light**: Maximize windows and skylights for brightness
- **Storage Solutions**: Include adequate storage in the new space
- **Utility Access**: Plan for electrical, plumbing, and HVAC needs

### Outdoor Integration

We consider how your addition affects outdoor spaces:

- **Landscaping**: Plan for minimal disruption to existing landscaping
- **Outdoor Access**: Include doors to patios, decks, or gardens
- **Privacy**: Consider window placement for privacy
- **Views**: Take advantage of scenic views from new spaces
