# Bathroom Renovation Services

Transform your bathroom into a personal oasis with our comprehensive renovation services. Whether you're updating a powder room or creating a luxury master suite, we deliver exceptional results that combine beauty, functionality, and lasting value.

## Our Bathroom Renovation Services

### Complete Bathroom Remodels

- **Full Renovations**: Complete transformation from floor to ceiling
- **Layout Modifications**: Optimize space and improve functionality
- **Accessibility Improvements**: Aging-in-place features and ADA compliance
- **Luxury Upgrades**: Spa-like features and high-end finishes

### Custom Tile Work

- **Floor Tile Installation**: Ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, and luxury vinyl
- **Wall Tile & Backsplashes**: Subway tile, mosaic patterns, and decorative accents
- **Shower Surrounds**: Custom tile work with proper waterproofing
- **Heated Floors**: Radiant floor heating for comfort and luxury

### Fixtures & Plumbing

- **Vanity Installation**: Custom and pre-fabricated vanities with storage solutions
- **Toilet Installation**: Standard, comfort height, and wall-mounted options
- **Shower & Tub Installation**: Walk-in showers, soaking tubs, and shower-tub combos
- **Plumbing Updates**: New supply lines, drain systems, and fixture connections

### Lighting & Ventilation

- **Vanity Lighting**: Stylish and functional lighting for grooming tasks
- **Recessed Lighting**: General and accent lighting throughout the space
- **Exhaust Fans**: Proper ventilation to prevent moisture and mold issues
- **Natural Light**: Skylights and window installations where possible

## The Bathroom Renovation Process

### 1. Design Consultation

We start with an in-home consultation to understand your needs, style preferences, and budget. We'll assess your current space and discuss possibilities for improvement.

### 2. Space Planning

Our design team creates detailed plans that optimize your bathroom layout for better functionality and flow. We consider plumbing locations, electrical needs, and ventilation requirements.

### 3. Material Selection

We help you choose materials that match your style and budget, from tiles and fixtures to lighting and hardware. We provide samples and 3D renderings to help you visualize the final result.

### 4. Permits & Preparation

We handle all necessary permits and prepare your space for renovation, including protecting surrounding areas and setting up temporary facilities if needed.

### 5. Construction & Installation

Our skilled craftsmen execute your renovation with attention to detail and quality workmanship. We coordinate all trades and keep you informed throughout the process.

### 6. Final Inspection

We conduct a thorough walkthrough to ensure every detail meets our standards and your expectations before considering the project complete.
